The Willow Table

Rooted in Community

What began as a small neighborhood café has grown into a community hub where friends and family come to share meals and memories. We partner with local farms and purveyors to ensure that every dish celebrates the best our region has to offer. Whether you're here for Sunday brunch or a cozy dinner, The Willow Table is your place to slow down and savor.
